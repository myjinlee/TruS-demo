Forgetting A Voice at Inference: Identity Unlearning for Zero-Shot TTS via Activation Steering

Jiyoung Lee1*, Co-author 22, Co-author 33, Co-author 44

1Ewha Womans University · 2NAVER AI Lab · 3Princeton University · 4NAVER CLOUD

Abstract

We propose an activation steering framework on top of the F5-TTS backbone, inspired by EmoSteer, to achieve speaker identity unlearning without retraining. Specifically, a steering vector is constructed from the activations of a single forget speaker and multiple remain speakers, normalized, and injected into selected layers and steps during inference. This suppresses the target speaker’s timbre contribution while preserving phonetic intelligibility. Experiments on Emilia and LibriSpeech show effective suppression of forget speakers with minimal degradation of remain and unseen speakers, demonstrating a practical, training-free approach to identity unlearning in zero-shot TTS.
